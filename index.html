Know about PW Skills

PW Skills - from the Physics Wallah family, is dedicated to helping aspirants upskill themselves and prepare for interviews for roles in Software Development, Business Analytics, Digital Marketing and Sales & Business Development. We will provide courses that focus on getting aspirants ready to crack interviews in those domains while also making sure that there is more than enough practice material and easy to understand content that even the beginners can begin their interview preparation journey.

PW Skills is already helping thousands of students to start upskilling who have subscribed to our Youtube Channel - College Wallah with a subscriber base of 2.7 Lakhs.

We Stand out because

We have a team of industry experts and ed-tech veterans who are creating industry relevant courses that focus on requisite Skills rather than general curriculum, for aspirants who want to get a job in various domains.

Our Key Focus Areas

PW Skill’s main focus is to help aspirants develop hard as well as soft skills which are in high demand in the present industry scenario. We want to ensure that all our students get access to high quality teaching & practice material and best possible educators who are invested in the students’ learning outcomes as much as the students.

What Makes us different

PW Skills is developing future-centric and upskilling focused courses to deliver best-in-class learning experience to our students at a cost which allows everyone with aptitude and a dream to be a part of India's biggest Upskilling revolution.
